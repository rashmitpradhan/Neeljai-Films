About The Brand

Neeljai Films is a full-service film production company that creates short films, ott web series, and feature films. A visionary in different forms of content creation, we try to produce impactful cinematic experiences that have the ability to keep the audience engaged on whichever device they are consuming the content from.

About the Founder

Mr. Pranay Garg, founder of Neeljai Films, started his career with theatre acting. Along with his acting stints, he produced his first play ‘ Tajmahal ka Tender’ which earned accolades and inspired him to take up production as a full time career. 

Now that his first ott series is live on Disney+ Hoststar, he plans to continue producing great content in terms of short films, web series and tv commercials and collaborate with talented artists to bring wonderful stories to the screen. 

Showcase

Crime Next Door

‘Crime Next Door’ is our first web series on Disney+ Hotstar. The story revolves around Inspector Shekhawat, who investigates some of the most heinous and toughest murder cases of his entire career. With actors like Anupriya Goenka, Girish Kulkarni, Rajendra Gupta, and Vatsal Sheth among the cast, this ott series has over 7 million views on the platform.
